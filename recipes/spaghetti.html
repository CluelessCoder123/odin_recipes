Spaghetti

Tender spaghetti noodles smothered in a rich, savory sauce. Easy to make, delicious to eat, and ideal for any occasion.

Ingredients

For this spaghetti, you will need the following ingredients.

  • 340g of any spaghetti - uncooked
  • 450ml of tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil - extra virgin
  • 5 large garlic cloves - minced
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons oregano - dried
  • Parmesan cheese - grated

Instructions

  1. Fill large Dutch oven or pot with cold water 3/4 full and bring to a boil. Add 2 teaspoons salt and spaghetti. Separate pasta with tongs a few times during first 2 minutes of cooking. This will ensure spaghetti do not stick together.
  2. Cook pasta uncovered for 5 more minutes or until al dente, stirring occasionally. Taste for doneness while cooking. Do not overcook until too soft. The key to tasty pasta is firm cooked pasta. Drain spaghetti in a colander.
  3. Return pot to medium heat and add olive oil, 4 garlic cloves and oregano. Turn off the heat. Stir constantly for 20 seconds.
  4. Add drained pasta, tomato sauce, remaining 1/2 teaspoon salt, black pepper and 1 garlic clove. Stir gently until warmed through.
  5. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and serve immediately.
